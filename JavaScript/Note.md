### Logical Operators

**AND(&&)**
| P | Q | P && Q |
|:-:|:-:| :---: |
|True|True| True|
|False|True| False|
|True|False| False|
|False| False| False|

---

**OR(||)**
| P | Q | P OR Q |
|:-:|:-:| :---: |
|True|True| True|
|False|True| True|
|True|False| True|
|False| False| False|

__Note__ That With **AND** both values must be true while **OR** either of the values must be true
